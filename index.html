More About Me

I was born in Frosinone, Italy, in 1993.
I received the Bachelor’s Degree in Computer Engineering from the University of Rome Tor Vergata in 2015 and the Master’s Degree in Robotics and Automation Engineering from the University of Pisa in 2019
I am currently a researcher at the University of Rome Tor Vergata, where I am involved in different projects about neural networks and drones.
In 2021 I was a member of Tor Vergata team that won the second prize at the 2021 edition of the Leonardo Drone Contest, where I worked as developer, tester and pilot.
My research interests deal with autonomous systems such as driverless cars, mobile robots and drones, and deep learning for image recognition.
In my free time I love taking pictures and playing volleyball.
